## Remediation Notes

Implemented after this review:

- Digest topic dedupe now collapses same-lifecycle social updates such as the Powell before/after pair.
- RSS Bloomberg video recap items such as `The Opening Trade` are skipped before entering the global digest pool.
- Personalized global stock-news picks with explicit tickers are filtered against the actor's holdings/watchlist/thesis symbols; symbolless macro/global context is still allowed.
- Analyst-grade events now surface price-target changes, for example `$360 -> $405`, and same-rating target changes are no longer treated as no-op holds.
- Close price moves below the high threshold are now Medium digest signals instead of Low, so a close move like VST -4.55% sorts above routine low-priority commentary.
